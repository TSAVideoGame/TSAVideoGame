Game Instructions:

The goal of this game is to collect all artifacts at each level by evading the guarding. If caught or “touched” by a guard, you will lose the level. 

Game controls:
Press ‘o’ to select options such as game levels and to exit out of the game over or game win screen
Press ‘p’ to pause the game, only if you are in a level

Player movement controls:
Press ‘w’ to move the player upwards
Press ‘a’ to move the player left
Press ‘s’ to move the player downwards
Press ‘d’ to move the player right
